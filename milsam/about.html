Website Design FAQs you might be interested in

FAQs

    Do you offer website hosting services?

    Yes. We offer website hosting services with email hosting services or integrating with Google Mail for businesses. Our ISP's have the state of the art technology and servers with accompanying  security infrastructure with affordable packages that meet your budget.

    Do you use website design templates in your works?

    No. Our creative designers in house do a great job of coming up with website designs from scratch that unifies your branding elements into a cohesive website design that best represent your brand and tone. In the case where for reasons such as budgetary constraint  or requirements we use a particular template or theme, we're able to adjust our workflow to accommodate such needs, even that we still portray your brand, colors, tone, etc. into the theme by doing the necessary customization.

    How can I be sure that you're the best website designer for my website development?

    Hiring a good website designer involves a number of factors. The few that you can consider are:

    1. Portfolio of the website designer or website design company
    2. The technologies or skill set of the web designer and or the team depending on the size of your project
    3. The number of years of relationship with existing clients
    4. The web designer's website :)

    For Kava, our portfolio cuts across a number of industries with a proven track record of developing websites for leading companies in Ghana.

    Can I update the website myself?

    Most of the websites we develop comes with a content management system (CMS) which allows you to update the website yourself. We offer training and consultation if requested to help train the Website Administration team.

    How long does it take you to design a website?

    The duration of the website design and development project depends on the requirements. We allow a minimum of 2-4 weeks to get deliverables. In special cases where the project needs a faster turn around other than 2 weeks, we're able to do so depending on the schedule and cost.

    What contents are required for my website?

    The copy or content for your website should mostly cover who you are, the products and services you offer, your processes, your team and portfolio and most importantly your contact information. These usually should come with the appropriate photos to accompany them. We offer content writing  and photography services as part of our effort to being a full service digital agency.

    How much would it cost to have a website designed for me?

    The cost of a website that is the design and development cost largely depends on the requirements of the project. We would be glad to give you a cost estimate when the details of the website design project are given. Do not hesitate to contact us or send us your information via our contact us page form.
