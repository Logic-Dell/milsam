Petra Trust

Corporate Website for Ghana's most recognized independent trustee company

About the Project

As a pension trustee startup company, Petra Trust was posed to position itself as one of the leading pension trustee companies in Accra, Ghana. Petra needed a website to reflects its brand position and to represents its vision to its prospects with simple and intuitive User Interface and Experience.

 

The need to get a corporate online presence brought them to Kava Media. The key to the success of the project to Kava was not just to address the business goals of the client but to communicate these goals in simple clear terms using the web as a medium to Petra’s diverse target audience. Our strategy was to focus on promoting their first online presence with an engaging user experience that will better reflects the company and its core services and products.
